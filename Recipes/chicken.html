    8. onion powder

Steps

    1. The first step is to make the spice mix that will coat the chicken. In a bowl, combine 1 teaspoon each of garam masala, turmeric, ground cumin, and ground coriander.
    2.Add 1 tablespoon of salt and mix everything together.Next, open the Licious Chicken Leg - Boneless Cubes pack and add it to the bowl of spices. Toss everything together so that the chicken is evenly coated.
    3.Now it's time to fry the chicken.
    4.Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces and cook for 5-7 minutes per side, or until they're golden brown and cooked through. Serve immediately with rice or naan bread. Enjoy! RECIPE NOTES
